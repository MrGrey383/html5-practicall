My Personal Journey

From curiosity to career: How I discovered my passion for technology and web development

Who I Am

I'm a dedicated Computer Science student at the United States International Univerisity, passionate about creating inclusive digital experiences. My journey in technology began with curiosity and has evolved into a mission to make the web accessible to everyone.

Currently in my fourth year of studies, I specialize in web development with a strong emphasis on UX design principles and accessibility standards.

Personal Values in Technology

My approach to development is guided by core principles that shape every project I work on:

Accessibility First
Every website should be usable by people with disabilities. I design and code with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and diverse user needs in mind from the very beginning.
Semantic Structure
Meaningful HTML creates better user experiences for everyone. I prioritize proper document structure that serves both humans and machines effectively.
Progressive Enhancement
Starting with solid HTML foundations ensures content remains accessible regardless of browser capabilities or network conditions.
Continuous Learning
Technology evolves rapidly, and I'm committed to staying current with best practices, emerging standards, and user feedback.
Community Impact
I believe technology should solve real problems and create opportunities for underrepresented communities to participate in the digital economy.
Ethical Development
Privacy, security, and user consent are fundamental considerations in every project I undertake.

Inspiration and Motivation

My passion for accessible web development was sparked by volunteering at a local community center where I saw firsthand how poorly designed websites excluded people from accessing essential services.

"Accessibility is a precondition for participation in society and the economy. It is both a human rights issue and a smart economic strategy."
— United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities

This experience reinforced my commitment to creating digital solutions that serve everyone, regardless of their abilities or circumstances.
